Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about admissions, programs, and facilities

Our admission process is simple and transparent. You can contact our admission office at +91 9973300414 or +91 6201686780 for detailed information. We also offer campus visits and counseling sessions to help you understand our programs better.

We offer various scholarship programs based on academic merit, financial need, and special talents. Our scholarship facility provides financial aid to eligible students to support their education and career aspirations.

Our school features smart classrooms with E-Boards and audio-visual aids, modern computer lab, well-equipped physics and chemistry laboratories, comprehensive library, and various other facilities for holistic education.

Our office hours are Monday to Friday from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM, and Saturday from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM. School timings may vary by grade level. Please contact us for specific class schedules.

You can schedule a campus visit by calling our office numbers or sending us an email. We encourage prospective students and parents to visit our campus to experience our learning environment firsthand.
