Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities at APJA Kalam High School

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

Your Privacy Matters

At APJA Kalam High School, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our students, parents, staff, and website visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you interact with our school and use our website.

Information We Collect

Student Information

  • Personal details: Name, date of birth, address, phone number
  • Parent/guardian contact information
  • Academic records, grades, and progress reports
  • Attendance records and disciplinary information
  • Medical information relevant to school activities
  • Photographs and videos for educational and promotional purposes
  • Emergency contact details

Parent/Guardian Information

  • Contact information: Name, address, phone number, email
  • Employment details for emergency purposes
  • Financial information for fee payments
  • Communication preferences

Website Visitors

  • IP address and browser information
  • Pages visited and time spent on our website
  • Contact form submissions and inquiries
  • Cookies and tracking data for website improvement

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing educational services and academic support
  • Communicating with students, parents, and guardians
  • Maintaining academic records and progress tracking
  • Ensuring student safety and security
  • Processing fee payments and financial transactions
  • Organizing school events and activities
  • Complying with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Improving our educational programs and services
  • Emergency communications and notifications
  • Creating yearbooks and promotional materials

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent personal information to third parties. We may share information only in these circumstances:

  • Educational Authorities: With education boards and government agencies as required
  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who help us operate our school (payment processors, technology providers)
  • Emergency Situations: To protect student safety and well-being
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Parent/Guardian Consent: With explicit consent for specific activities
  • School Transfers: When students transfer to other educational institutions

Data Security and Protection

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

  • Secure servers with limited access controls
  • Encryption of sensitive data during transmission
  • Regular security audits and system updates
  • Staff training on data protection protocols
  • Physical security of paper records and files
  • Secure backup and recovery systems
  • Multi-factor authentication for administrative access
  • Regular monitoring for unauthorized access attempts

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request copies of personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of personal information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Portability: Request transfer of information to another institution
  • Restriction: Request limitation of how we use your information
  • Objection: Object to certain uses of your personal information
  • Communication Preferences: Opt-out of non-essential communications

Student Privacy Protection

We are committed to protecting student privacy and comply with all applicable education privacy laws:

  • We collect only information necessary for educational purposes
  • Student records are kept confidential and secure
  • Access to student information is limited to authorized personnel
  • We obtain proper consent before using student photos or videos
  • Students under 18 require parental consent for certain activities
  • We follow strict guidelines for student data retention and disposal

Cookies and Website Technology

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for website functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand website usage patterns
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Third-party Cookies: From embedded content (videos, maps)

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

Data Retention

We retain personal information for different periods depending on the type of data:

  • Student Academic Records: As required by educational regulations (typically 7-10 years)
  • Financial Records: As required by accounting and tax laws
  • Medical Records: As required by healthcare regulations
  • Website Analytics: Typically 24-36 months
  • Communication Records: As necessary for ongoing relationships

Updates to This Policy

  • Sending notification to registered email addresses
  • Announcing changes through school communication channels
  • Providing notice as required by applicable laws
